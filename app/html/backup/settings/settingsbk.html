Welcome to the Calendly Extension by Xtracut Systems!
Our extension is designed to provide you with a seamless experience for managing your appointments and scheduling. We've incorporated two dropdown menus that offer you flexibility and control over your workflow.
The first dropdown menu allows you to choose the destination for adding new person contacts. With a simple selection, you can ensure that your contacts are organized efficiently within your preferred system.
The second dropdown menu empowers you to decide whether new appointments should be added to tasks or meetings. Tailor this setting to match your specific workflow and optimize your scheduling process.
